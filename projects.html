Honeycomb

A self-healing peer to peer messaging network for disaster conditions.

Built around a Chord-inspired ring and stress tested on a 70 node EC2 setup to measure recovery when failures hit mid run.

  • Chord style ring with successor lists and a long range finger pointer
  • Probabilistic broadcast forwarding with message ID deduplication
  • Failure injection experiments tracking convergence and delivery behavior

Travel Journal
