StaySignals

Built for the Klaviyo Winter Challenge hackathon, where I won $10,000 as a solo participant. A concierge workflow prototype for boutique hotels: pick a guest, see their timeline, and generate staff-ready concierge briefs and responses. It reads guest context from Klaviyo (Profiles + Events) and uses Gemini to turn messy event data into structured, actionable notes. Designed as a simple, fast UI that makes “who is this guest and what do they need” feel obvious.

WinePair

I worked on the full stack build (FastAPI + Azure SQL + React/Tailwind) and the server-side pairing pipeline so results load quickly and consistently.

Slam Poetry Generator

A generative poetry system that writes short poems, performs them out loud, and only saves the ones that score well. Under the hood it is a Markov chain trained on Teasdale and H.D., plus a lightweight evaluator that rewards vivid imagery, basic structure, and rhythm while punishing repetition. There is also a small Flask web mode where the poem types in line-by-line while Safari voices perform it live.
